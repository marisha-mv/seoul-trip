A few useful things.

Book ahead

  • Octobee Studio — WhatsApp at least a week ahead. They book hair/makeup partner salon too.
  • Park Jun Cheongdam — Instagram DM 5–7 days ahead. Two people, English-speaking designer.
  • Joo 052, Vinho — CATCHTABLE or call. Vinho fills up.
  • Standard Bread — CATCHTABLE the night before.
  • Olive Young skin scan, Smart Skn — walk-in possible but appointment better.
  • OWM — no booking needed, just walk in.

Post-Heal rules

  • No saunas / jjimjilbang for 48 hrs after Botox. Bathhouse Wednesday is fine.
  • No alcohol 24hrs after — skip wine at Vinho.
  • No facial massage / heat for 24hrs. Body massage Wed is fine.
  • Hair spa Wednesday = 40+ hrs post-Heal. Safe.
  • Sleep slightly elevated Monday night.
  • SPF 50, reapply, especially walking days.
